GAME DEV

Core Programming Concepts

This course offers an intensive overview of data structures and algorithms as applied to the game development process. The participants will be introduced to ways in which effective structuring of data and algorithms can help increase the efficiency of games in terms of their performance, reactivity and scalability. Some topics to be covered in this course include the application of data structures in organizing the state of a game, its entities, and its resources, as well as the application of algorithms in such aspects of games as navigation, collision detection, physics, and AI.

Career Opportunity

High-Performance Embedded Systems

THaving good understanding of the concepts of data structures and algorithms provides numerous job opportunities in the game development field and other industries. One may become a Game Programmer, Gameplay Engineer, Technical Game Designer, AI Programmer, Engine Developer, and Graphics Programmer. Besides, the analytical thinking and problem solving skills acquired during the course provide the necessary prerequisites for becoming an applicant for jobs in software engineering and technical consulting. Due to the growth of the gaming industry, there is a great need for people who have good knowledge of algorithms.
